WiFi Connection

  1. Place your sensor in WiFi listening mode by holding the SETUP button down until the LED blinks blue.
  2. Your sensor is now broadcasting its name via WiFI. Connect your device to the WiFi network named Photon-XXXX.
  3. When you have connected, click the SCAN button below.

Don't see your network? Move me closer to your router, then re-scan.
